Television Energy Consumption

Televisions are a common household appliance, and their energy consumption can vary significantly based on technology (e.g., LED, OLED, QLED), screen size, and usage patterns.

In Australia, the Energy Rating Label helps consumers compare the energy efficiency of different models. Look for a higher star rating to choose a more efficient television.
